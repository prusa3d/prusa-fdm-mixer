Prusa FDM Mixer · color prediction for multi-color 3D printing

A calibrated model that predicts what your interleaved filament mix will actually look like on the print bed — within ΔE 5.7 median on 107 measured Prusa XL samples.

Playground

Drag a slider, watch the predicted color update live. 2-color or 3-color triangle.

Harness

Score the model against measured prints. Compare against linear RGB, Kubelka-Munk, and PolyMixer.

Gatherer

Add your own measurements (LAB from a colorimeter or eyeball). Export JSONL.

For slicer developers

The model is available as a TypeScript module (src/filament-mix.ts) and as a drop-in C++17 implementation (cpp/filament_mix.{hpp,cpp}). MIT licensed — vendor it.
