About these predictions

The v7 model was fitted on 107 measured 2-color samples (Prusa XL, PLA). Median error ΔE 5.7; ~45% under the JND threshold of ΔE 5. 3-color predictions extend the model linearly with limited validation data — useful for ranking ideas, not for committing to a 20-hour print.
